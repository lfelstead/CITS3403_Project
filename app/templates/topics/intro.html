Cant RESIST Learning?

Resistors are electronic components that are included in circuits to reduce current flow and lower voltage levels. The primary use of a resistor is to manipulate the current flowing through a circuit to levels that the active components require. Each resistor has a set value of resistance measured in Ohms (Ω).


Resistor behaviour is dictated by the physical relationship of Ohm’s law in which:

V=I×R

Where V = Voltage, I = Current, and R = resistance.
